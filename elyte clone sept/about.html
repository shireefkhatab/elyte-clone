Our Story

NetBits is one of the leading companies within the UK in Providing IT services. We provide consulting services in partnerships, outsourcing and support to all major companies and organisations that value and recognise the use of telecommunication networks an indispensable tool for their business. Over the past 15 years of consulting, Netbits has been able to operate in diverse environment, by developing an internal know how in the day today world of Information Communication technology to meet new challenges in modern day businesses. As a whole Netbits is a constantly evolving substantiality, both in terms of services offered and business goals. The primary mission of Netbits has always been the sale of value added services based on skills and experience of its staff, aimed to provide full support to the needs and issues of clients. Netbits is solely a company of people, who continuously invest in the training and certification of its staff, who have acquired decades of experience developed in their senior consultants to design and frame telecommunication strategies. It’s a progress that bases its strength and success on customer satisfaction going hand in hand with the quality achievements and expertise of its human resources and manpower.
